Robonova AI 3 - Humanoid Robot Platform

Khwaja Moinuddin Chishti Language University, Lucknow

Overview

Robonova AI 3 is an advanced humanoid robot developed by Scientech. It is designed for educational, research, and prototyping purposes. Featuring 17 servo motors and embedded AI processing capabilities, this robot can perform complex movements and tasks such as object tracking, obstacle avoidance, and interactive demonstrations.

Hardware Specifications

  • Robot Body: 16-joint bipedal structure with high-grade aluminum parts
  • Servo Motors: 17 x HSR-8498 digital servos
  • Controller Board: MR-C3024, controls up to 32 servos
  • Processor: Amlogic Cortex-A53 Quad-Core 1.5GHz with Mali-450 GPU
  • Memory: 2GB DDR3 SDRAM
  • Camera: Full HD 1920x1080 camera module
  • Connectivity: Bluetooth, Gigabit Ethernet, HDMI 2.0, 40-pin GPIO
  • Power: 2900mAh Li-ion rechargeable battery
  • Weight/Size: 1.3 kg, 310 x 180 x 90 mm

Advantages

  • Integrated AI and vision system

Limitations

  • Relatively high cost

  • Proprietary components harder to replace
